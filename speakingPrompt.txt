Explanation of IELTS Speaking Evaluation:

The IELTS Speaking session is scored from 0 to 9 by trained examiners. Your Speaking is assessed based on Task Achievement, Coherence & Cohesion, Lexical Resource, and Grammatical Range & Accuracy. These criteria measure how well you address the task, organize your ideas, use vocabulary, and apply grammar. The average of these scores determines your overall band score.
